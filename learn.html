Piano

The piano is a musical instrument with a keyboard that produces sound by striking strings with hammers. It has 88 keys, including 52 white keys and 36 black keys, arranged in a repeating pattern of groups of two and three.

Additionally, it features pedals for various effects. Renowned for its versatility, it's used across genres and offers benefits like improved coordination and cognitive abilities through learning.

Middle C

Middle C is a specific musical note that is often used as a reference point in music notation and theory.

On the piano keyboard, middle C is located roughly in the middle of the keyboard. It is the white key immediately to the left of a group of two black keys when looking at the keyboard.

Middle C on the musical staff is typically positioned on a ledger line below the treble staff or on a ledger line above the bass staff.

Time Signature

The time of a pice of music is shown by a Time Signature which consists of 2 numbers writen above the other. The time signature is written immediatedlt after the key signature at the beginning of the melody.

The top number of a time signature shows how many beats there are in a bar.

E.g. There are 4 beats(crotchet) in each bar.
